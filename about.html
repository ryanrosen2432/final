About

The Lakers franchise started in 1948 when they were called the Minneapolois Lakers. They went on to win 5 titles in 6 seasons in Minneapolis before moving to the forum stadium in Los Angeles. The Lakers have had many greats in their past like Wilt Chamberlain, Jerry West, Magic Johnson, Kareem, Shaq, and Kobe. The Lakers are one of the most successful franchises in sports and have won 16 championships all time which is second most in the NBA.
